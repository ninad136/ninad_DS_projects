Problem Statement :¶

Analyse user survey data from Mechanical Turk to understand the factors influencing coupon acceptance decisions for various dining establishments (Restaurant, Coffee House, or Bar) during different driving scenarios, enabling improved coupon distribution strategies and user engagement. The survey describes different driving scenarios including the user’s destination, current time, weather, passenger, coupon attributes, user attributes, and contextual attributes, and then asks the user whether he/she will accept the coupon or not.
